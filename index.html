Ticketing Master, Artist & Audiovisual Producer

Emilio (Milo) Miranda (b. 1994, Mexico City) works at the intersection of photography, video, and digital storytelling across Mexico and the Netherlands.

He experiments with 3D scanning, CCTV, and virtual modeling to create immersive narratives exploring identity, cultural heritage, and migration.
