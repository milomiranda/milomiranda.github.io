About

Creative Content Producer with a Master’s in Digital Culture Studies (Tilburg University) and 4 years of experience in the Netherlands, specializing in storytelling across fashion, media, and cultural industries. Skilled in developing and producing content that blends photography, film, live events, podcasts, and social media activations. My work has been featured in Vogue Italia PhotoVogue, Neo2 Magazine, Kaltblut, RedBull, Spotify, and H&M, and I have produced campaigns and programs for E! Entertainment, Estée Lauder, H&M, and Ermenegildo Zegna. Known for transforming creative ideas into impactful productions that engage audiences across TV, digital platforms, and live experiences.
