Christian Chavez for Hombre Testarudo Magazine, Mexico City, MX

Produced a fashion editorial for the Mexican magazine Hombre Testarudo, featuring former actor and singer Christian Chávez. The shoot took place at the exclusive St. Regis Residences in downtown Mexico City, blending luxury aesthetics with contemporary styling. This project was published as the cover story of the magazine, accompanied by an extended spread inside.
