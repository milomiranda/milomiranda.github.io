"Durga", directed by Milo Miranda, produced by ANCI, soundtrack for "Mantram", a collective album, Brazil, Brazil

An audiovisual study on the Goddess Kali. MANTRAM is a collective album, featuring the special participation of experimental electronic music producers: Biophillick, MVST, Milo Miranda, djalgoritmo, Phoebe, euFraktus X, Todas Tus Caras. Inspired by the states of the Hindu divinity KALI, each author, from within their own creative-experimental universe, opens their intuition to unfold an audiovisual study of the GODDESS. This gives rise to electronic compositions that invite imagination, reflection, calmness, or even meditation. Producing electronic MANTRAMS that engage in dialogue with spirituality and intuition through sound.

Inspired by the states of the divinity of Hinduism KALI, “DURGA” appears, a track directed by Milo Miranda produced by ANCI. In the book markandeia purana, a goddess Kalika is described. He mentions that it was emanating from the forehead of the goddess Durga (the "inaccessible" demon slayer).
