"Step into U" an exhibition from Miguel Gori at ARC Amsterdam

Arc Amsterdam asked Miguel Gori to create a project that would connect with the Arc Amsterdam community, and the result is "U". With his signature eye for detail, style, and aesthetics, Miguel captured portraits of people within the ARC Network, revealing their essence and personality in every frame. Each portrait offers a window into hidden stories, filled with intensity and nuance that invite reflection. These aren’t just images; they are living narratives, each one unique, powerful, and full of character.

It's all about identity, expression, and the beauty of what makes us "U"nique. A project by creative director and photographer Miguel Gori, former art director Vogue Netherlands.
