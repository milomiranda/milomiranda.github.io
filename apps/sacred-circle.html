The Ritual
Step I — Preparation
Gather all ingredients. Light your incense. Stand in the East. Take three deep breaths and set your intention.
Step II — Trace the Circle
Starting from the East, walk clockwise. Complete three full rounds. Visualize a blue bubble of light forming a boundary between you and the outside.
Step III — Consecration of Air
East · Air
"With the perfumed air, I consecrate this circle"
Step IV — Consecration of Fire
South · Fire
"With the sacred fire, I consecrate this circle"
Step V — Consecration of Water
West · Water
Take your cup of water and move to the West.
"With the water of sky and rivers, I consecrate this circle"
Step VI — Consecration of Earth
North · Earth
"With the salt of the earth, I consecrate this circle"
Step VII — Release
Walk one round counter-clockwise. Give thanks to all present energies.
"I thank the universe for this circle, and return the energies to their source. All is now as before."
