Milo Miranda

Milo Miranda (b. 1994, Mexico City) is an artist and content producer working at the intersection of photography, video, and digital manipulation. With a background in Digital Culture Studies and years of experience across Mexico, the Netherlands, Germany, and Spain, his practice explores themes of identity, cultural heritage, and migration.

Combining experimental and performative approaches, Milo merges traditional image-making with emerging technologies—3D scanning, CCTV, and virtual modeling—to create works that challenge perceptions of belonging and presence. His projects function as both artistic research and visual storytelling, producing immersive narratives that invite connection while questioning how cultural memory survives in a digitized world.
