About

Emilio (Milo) Miranda (b. 1994, Mexico City) is an artist and content producer working at the intersection of photography, video, and digital storytelling. With a background in Digital Culture Studies and experience across Mexico and the Netherlands, he collaborates with clients and brands to ground ideas and bring them to life, producing brand activations, marketing campaigns, television programs and series, music projects, photography, and film, as well as providing business consultancy to help shape creative strategies.

In his personal work, Milo experiments with merging traditional image-making and emerging technologies—such as 3D scanning, CCTV, and virtual modeling—to create immersive narratives that explore identity, cultural heritage, and migration. His projects function as both artistic research and visual storytelling, questioning how cultural memory survives in a digitized world.
