Vigilante #1" & "Vigilante #2" at BetweenYOU&ME, Tilburg, The Netherlands

The inspiration for this project emerged after analyzing the socio-psychological impact of watching a video captured by surveillance cameras, the world of surveillance increases over time in public and private spaces. The two videos recorded with surveillance cameras show two different situations, the first situation portrays the interaction between a man and a woman. The second situation tells of a person suffering from anxiety and stress in everyday life."

The videos created are being exhibited on a led screen located in the city center of Tilburg, the platform called "Between you & me" will play the videos 1,344 times within six weeks.
